Who are your competitors? What do you understand about your business that they don't?

>> Now: JetBrains DataBao, Altimate Code, Hex (on analysis) are major competitions for now. We don’t view generalized harnesses (claude code, cursor, codex etc) as competition as we are enriching the auto optimized context and adding data specific toolsets to these harnesses, effectively making us complementary.

Future: any company seating between data warehouse and user data consumption (via slack / notion / dashboard) is longer horizon competition. (PowerBI, Tableau, ThoughSpot, Mode etc).

We categorize our edge in two categories:
1.Relatively newer agent-native companies: we have already proved superior context management and performance with the Spider 2.0 benchmark. Our AutoFyn harness optimizer, which has been our secret weapon optimizing the harness for custom deployment to teams. This scales nicely long term in our vision to be the data context layer and governance layer for the data team and data consumers.

2. For the relatively older / legacy companies: They are building a “co-pilot” for their product with a simple chat based interface to retrofit their products. But 2026 and beyond these product substrates might become obsolete. For example: people used Hex or Mode (or ThoughtSpot) for manually writing code or building would likely just fire off their report generation and dashboarding literally from slack or other messaging apps, completely bypassing the need for the intermediate substrate.
