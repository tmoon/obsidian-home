# JetBrains Databao

Prior #1 on [[Spider 2.0-DBT]] with **44.11**. Now runner-up. As of 2026-04-21, **+7.45 below SignalPilot** (per blog and repo README).

## Significance

JetBrains is a category-leading dev-tools incumbent with deep IDE and data-tool footprint (DataGrip, IntelliJ DataSpell, etc.). Beating them on a public leaderboard is a credibility unlock for SignalPilot's GTM, particularly with data/platform teams who already trust JetBrains' brand.

## How to use this in positioning

- **Use:** "We're #1 on Spider 2.0-DBT — by 7+ points over JetBrains' Databao." (Specific, verifiable, anchored.)
- **Avoid:** Anything that mocks or punches down at JetBrains. They're a respectable incumbent and may become a partner / acquirer down the road.
- **Sequence:** Lead with the score and gap; only reference JetBrains by name when the listener already understands the leaderboard context.
