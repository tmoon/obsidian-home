### Ingredients
1. Aromatics: 
	1. garlic
	2. onion
	3. green pepper / jalapinio (for heat)
	4. carrot
	5. ginger
	6. lemon and zest
2. sweet corn 
3. stock / water
4. (optional) spices 
	1. cumin
	2. red pepper flakes
	3. garam masala
	4. cayenne / paprika
	5. 


Other variants:
1. celery for more aromatic flavors
2. bacon for richness
3. cup of heavy cream / milk for added richness
4. potatoes for chowder style creaminess
5. corn starch or egg drop for thickening
6. spices for adding warmth
7. New orleans style, add corn at the end (#5)
