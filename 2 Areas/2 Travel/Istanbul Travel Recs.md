### Sites to Visit
1. Topkapi palace: Ottoman palace with a great museum
2. Hagia Sophia: former church and current mosque
3. Blue mosque
4. Suleymaniye Mosque
5. Galata Tower
6. Grand bazaar
7. Istanbul Archeological Museum: Lots of greco-Roman art pieces
8.  İstiklal Avenue (old Taksim Square)

### Things to do
1. Walk around in the old istanbul area (including the bazaar)
2. Eat local food (avoid eating in the old istanbul as it's primarily a tourist trap)
3. Take a ferry boat on the Bosporus (public ferry id)
4. Visit İstiklal Avenue (old Taksim Square)
5. DOĞACIYIZ GOURMET: One of the best breakfast spots

### Food to Eat
1. Hafize Mustafa (they have a bunch of branches): incredible turkish desserts, try fig pudding
2. Şehzade Cağ Kebap: One of the best kebab shops
3. Köşkeroğlu Karaköy
4. Karaköy Lokantası
