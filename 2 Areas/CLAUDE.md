# Areas — Ongoing Responsibilities (Tier 1 vs Tier 2)

> **Function:** Life domains that never finish. Maintained at standards, not pushed to a goal state.

Areas are NOT projects. Projects happen *within* areas (e.g., "Run NYC Half" project lives within "Health & Wealth" area).

---

## Tier 1 — actively maintain (founder leverage)

The areas with `1` or `0` prefix in this folder. These directly affect whether you can ship product and convert pipeline.

- **[0 SignalPilot](0%20SignalPilot/)** — company artifacts (people, docs, ops)
- **[1 Health and Wealth](1%20Health%20and%20Wealth/)** — sleep, exercise, energy, runway
- **[1 Productivity](1%20Productivity/)** — systems, tools, workflows
- **[1 Reading](1%20Reading/)** — focused inputs (no infinite scroll)

> **Founder trap:** neglect Health & Wealth → burn out → window slips. The 60-day Spider 2.0 window only converts if you stay in the chair.

---

## Tier 2 — minimum maintenance only

The `2`-prefix areas. Maintained to standards; not actively optimized.

[2 Cooking](2%20Cooking/) · [2 Home Improvements](2%20Home%20Improvements/) · [2 Organization](2%20Organization/) · [2 Taxes](2%20Taxes/) · [2 Travel](2%20Travel/) · [20 Houses and Real Estate](20%20Houses%20and%20Real%20Estate/)

If a Tier-2 starts demanding weekly attention, ask: should it be promoted, or should a project be created to stabilize it?

## Rules

- **Weekly:** pick **1 tier-1 area** to deepen this week. Write one sentence: *"Next Sunday I want to be able to say _____."*
- **Monthly:** scan tier-1 health. Anything degrading → start a project to fix.
- **Quarterly:** archive any tier-2 area neglected 90+ days with no pain. Free the cognitive load.

---

## Area vs project decision

> Will this ever be done?
>
> - Yes → Project ([1 Projects](../1%20Projects/))
> - No → Area (here)

---

## AI behavior

- When working on an Area, log significant work in [Weekly Work Log](../5%20Weekly%20Work%20Logs/).
- Default: don't touch tier-2 areas unless explicitly asked.
- Push back when the user starts optimizing a tier-2 area mid-credibility-window — it's almost always procrastination.
