## Rules

**Weekly Focus:**
- Pick 3 Areas needing attention this week
- Ignore the other 5 (they're fine for now)
- Rotate focus based on what needs care

**Monthly Review:**
- Check health of each Area
- Any neglected >60 days? Address or archive
- Standards slipping? Create project to fix

**Archive When:**
- No longer a responsibility (e.g., sold house)
- Can merge with another Area
- No activity 60+ days despite being important

---

## Area vs Project Decision

**Ask:** "Will this ever be done?"

- **Yes** â†’ It's a Project (goes in Projects folder)
- **No** â†’ It's an Area (lives here)

**Example:**
- "Health & Wealth" Area â†’ ongoing responsibility
- "Run marathon in <4hrs" Project â†’ happens within Health Area
- "Hit $1M ARR" Project â†’ happens within Work/Company Area
