### N Body Problem of Turkish Suffix
	
1. Subject:
	1. Pronouns (and relevant suffix change)
			1. Singular: Ben, Sen/Siz, O -> Suffix: -um/-im, -sin/-siniz/-sun/-sunuz, -
		1. Plural: Biz, Sen/Siz, Onlar -> Suffix: -uz, -sun,sunuz, -lar, -ler
	2. Possessive: (My, Your, her)
		1. Singular: Benim, Senin/Sizin, Onun
		2. Plural: Bizim, Senin/Sizin, Onlarin
		3. Self: Kendi + suffix e.g. kendim, kendin, kendimiz, kendileri 
		4. Pronouns (ki): benim-ki (mine)
1. Object: 
	1. Singular or plural: -ler / -lar
	2. accussative the: -i / -u e.g. kalem-i (the pen)
2. Infinitive state (to do -> yakmak): -mak / -mek 
3. Tenses: 
	1. Present: - - + subject suffix e.g. Ben içim
	2. Present continuous: -yor- (+ subject suffix) e.g. Ben gidiyorum
	3. Past: -di- / -du-
	4. Past Continuous: -du- + yor- + 
	5. Future / Future Continuous: -acak / -ecek-
4. Advanced Teneses:
	1. No: -mi- + tense suffix + subject suffix e.g. Ben içmiyorum
