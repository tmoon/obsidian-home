# Bangladesh NVR Application

## Project Overview
This project outlines the steps required to obtain a No Visa Required (NVR) endorsement for travel to Bangladesh through the Consulate General of Bangladesh in New York.

## Archived Tasks (October 27, 2025)

### Eligibility Verification
- [x] Confirm eligibility for NVR:
  - [x] Foreign citizen of Bangladeshi origin (excluding SAARC countries).

### Document Preparation
- [ ] Complete the NVR Application Form:
  - [ ] Fill out the online form at [visa.gov.bd](https://visa.gov.bd) and print the version with the barcode.
  - [ ] Alternatively, download and fill out the form manually from the Consulate's website: https://bdcgny.org/uploads/files/NVR_Application_Form_new.pdf
- [ ] Gather required documents:
  - [ ] Original valid U.S. passport and a photocopy of the photo page.
  - [ ] Proof of Bangladeshi origin, such as:
    - [ ] Photocopy of the photo page of a Bangladeshi passport (Machine Readable or handwritten).
    - [ ] Bangladeshi Birth Registration Certificate.
  - [ ] One recent passport-sized photograph.
	  - [ ] Print / find something on file
  - [ ] NVR Payment:
    - [ ] A money order, postal order, or bank-certified check for $80.00 made payable to the "Consulate General of Bangladesh in New York".
    - [ ] Go get this from Chase Banl

### Submission
- [ ] Submit the application and required documents to the Consulate General of Bangladesh in New York.
  - [ ] Ensure all documents are complete and accurate.
  - [ ] Do not send original documents except for the passport on which the NVR will be endorsed.

### Follow-Up
- [ ] Track the application status through the Consulate or online portal.
- [ ] Collect the passport with the NVR endorsement once approved.
