## Coach behaviors

**Operator side (default):**
- *"What ships in 60 minutes?"* before *"what should we plan?"*
- Name the bottleneck: supply (build) or demand (distribute)? Most weeks: distribute.
- Refuse fake work: "refactor / research more / explore options" → procrastination.
- Direct, not soft. No congratulations for shipping the obvious.

**Strategist side (override):**
- *"Is this in the blue ocean or the red?"* before committing >1 day.
- *"What would invalidate the thesis?"* — pressure test monthly.
- *"What did the red-ocean players ship this month? What didn't they touch?"*
- Refuse to grind on a strategy that hasn't moved the funnel in 2 weeks. Re-evaluate.

## Daily founder rhythm (operator mode)

- **07:00–09:30 CREATE** — code, copy, pitch. Phone airplane mode. ONE frog task.
- **10:00–13:00 REPLY** — DMs, comments, intros. <24h response is brand. >48h is lost lead.
- **14:00–17:00 DISTRIBUTE** — push outward. Without distribution, AM creation = 0 value.
- **17:00–19:00 MEET** — customer / partner / fundraising calls.
- **21:00 REFLECT** — 3 bullets to [Weekly Work Log](5%20Weekly%20Work%20Logs/). Pick tomorrow's frog.

> **90-min rule:** no shipped output in 90 minutes → stop, change context, walk.

## Sunday weekly review (30 min)

1. **0–10 min — Inbox** to zero. Tag captures by Land/Expand/Speak/Write/Partner.
2. **10–20 min — Triage Running.** No deliverable in 2 weeks → archive. Pick 1–3 for next week.
3. **20–25 min — Funnel metrics.** Update PMF Dashboard: stars Δ, installs Δ, AutoFyn intros Δ, outbound DMs, content shipped, hot accounts.
4. **25–30 min — Blue-ocean check + intention.** *"Did this week's work compound toward the Automated Data Stack vision, or was it red-ocean drift?"* Pick 1 Area to deepen. Write: *"Next Sunday I want to be able to say _____."*

## Monthly research block (strategist mode, 60 min, last Sunday)

Run after the weekly review. This is the Chief-of-Staff slot.

1. **Competitor scan** — new entries on Spider 2.0 leaderboard? New dbt-AI products? New MCP data agents shipped this month?
2. **ICP recheck** — are seed–Series A dbt shops still the sharpest pain? Any new buyer segment surfacing?
3. **Thesis pressure test** — if you stopped shipping for a week and just thought, would the wedge change? What would *invalidate* the Automated Data Stack hypothesis?
4. **Blue-ocean re-map** — what did Cursor / Windsurf / Devin / Claude Code ship this month? What didn't they touch? Is our wedge still uniquely ours?

Findings go into [the wiki](1%20Projects/0%20Running%20Projects/SignalPilot%20New%20Direction/index.md) as raw sources / new entities. Each finding is an ingest opportunity.

## Decision framework

For any task / idea / capture, in order:

1. Does this move the funnel? (Land/Expand/Speak/Write/Partner) → if no, archive.
2. Is this in the blue ocean? → if it could be done by a frontier-lab copilot, **flag it** before committing.
3. <2 minutes? → do NOW.
4. Will it matter in 30 days? → if no, downgrade.
5. Who else can do it? → delegate.
6. Will it ship something visible? → upgrade.
7. Am I procrastinating on a harder thing? → do that instead.

## Emergency protocols

- **5+ Running projects** → archive everything not Land/Expand/Speak/Write/Partner. Pick max 1 of each.
- **Channel cold** → switch channels. Distribution is multi-channel by design.
- **Burned out** → 24h fully off. Window is 60 days; pacing matters.
- **Lost tomorrow** → open [PMF Dashboard](1%20Projects/0%20Running%20Projects/SignalPilot%20New%20Direction/PMF%20Dashboard.md). Pick the bottleneck.
- **Tempted to refactor** → distribution is the bottleneck. Don't refactor your way out of outbound.
- **Red-ocean drift** → 3+ shipped items this month feel commodity? Stop. Run an emergency strategist session. Re-anchor to the blue-ocean wedge.

## Health metrics

| Status | Operator signals | Strategist signals |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | Stars +20/wk, installs +10/wk, 3+ DMs/day, 1+ piece shipped/wk, weekly review done | Monthly market scan run; thesis still passes pressure test |
| 🟡 Yellow | Stars/installs flat 2 wks, <1 DM/day, missed 1 weekly review | Skipped one monthly scan; thesis untested |

- Operator-Red → stop building. Send 5 outbound DMs before opening any code.
- Strategist-Red → stop shipping. Run the monthly research block today, even if it's not Sunday.
