## Daily founder rhythm

- **07:00–09:30 CREATE** — code, copy, pitch. Phone airplane mode. ONE frog task.
- **10:00–13:00 REPLY** — DMs, comments, intros. <24h response is brand. >48h is lost lead.
- **14:00–17:00 DISTRIBUTE** — push outward. Without distribution, AM creation = 0 value.
- **17:00–19:00 MEET** — customer / partner / fundraising calls.
- **21:00 REFLECT** — 3 bullets to [Weekly Work Log](5%20Weekly%20Work%20Logs/). Pick tomorrow's frog.

> **90-min rule:** no shipped output in 90 minutes → stop, change context, walk. Movement > sitting.

## Sunday weekly review (30 min, non-negotiable)

1. **0–10 min** — Inbox to zero. Tag captures by Land/Expand/Speak/Write/Partner.
2. **10–20 min** — Triage Running. No deliverable in 2 weeks → archive. Pick 1–3 for next week.
3. **20–25 min** — Update PMF metrics in Weekly Work Log: stars Δ, installs Δ, AutoFyn intros Δ, outbound DMs sent, content shipped, hot accounts.
4. **25–30 min** — Pick 1 Area to deepen. Write: *"Next Sunday I want to be able to say _____."*

## Decision framework

For any task / idea / capture, in order:

1. Does this move the funnel? (Land/Expand/Speak/Write/Partner) → if no, archive
2. <2 minutes? → do NOW
3. Will it matter in 30 days? → if no, downgrade
4. Who else can do it? → delegate
5. Will it ship something visible? → upgrade
6. Am I procrastinating on a harder thing? → do that instead

## Emergency protocols

- **5+ projects** → archive everything not Land/Expand/Speak/Write/Partner. Pick max 1 of each.
- **Channel cold** → switch channels. Don't grind.
- **Burned out** → 24h fully off. Window is 60 days; pacing matters.
- **Lost tomorrow** → open [PMF Dashboard](1%20Projects/0%20Running%20Projects/SignalPilot%20New%20Direction/PMF%20Dashboard.md). Pick the bottleneck.
- **Tempted to refactor** → distribution is the bottleneck. Don't refactor your way out of outbound.
